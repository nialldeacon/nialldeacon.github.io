COVID-19 Precautions and Regulations

General COVID-19 Regulations in Germany

There are two very basic things to know about Corona rules in Germany.

  1. Indoor public spaces plus all public transport and public transport stops/stations require medical or FFP2 masks
  2. Bars, restaurants, hotels, museums and hairdressers require that guests meet one of the 3Gs:
    • Geimpfft - Fully Vaccinated with at least two weeks since the second dose and in possession of a vaccination certificate
    • Genesen - Recovered from COVID-19 with a formal certificate stating this
    • Getestet - Have certified negative test from a test centre within the last 24 hours. This can be a PCR or lateral flow test.
    The main exception to the rules above is that nightclubs and discos require a certified PCR test not more than 24 hours old.
